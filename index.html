About

VALORANT is a tactical shooter where you and up to 4 teammates team up to fight against 5 agents, with one life per round, 13 total! It sounds exciting, right? But VALORANT isn't just about shooting skills. You'll also have to use your special abilities, strategy, and wit to defeat (in style) your opponents while also planting or detonating bombs.

You may have heard us talk about servers or regions. Both of these are about where you are in the world! For example, if you're joining the game from North America for the first time. Your server will be NA. Now you might be wondering what the benefits of servers are. The answer is that VALORANT signaling is a sensitivity test where lag cannot be tolerated. Your server will connect you to the closest server to help reduce lag. Remember, you cannot change the server with your region. So please use extra caution if you play through a VPN.

There are eight ranks. Each rank is divided into three tiers: Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Immortal, and our highest rank is VALORANT. Winning the competition is the key to climbing the ranks. But if you play better than expected, then You will be able to climb up the ranks faster. which in contrast If you lose and play worse than expected (compared to the previous round) your rank will decrease.

Agents are playable characters in VALORANT that are modeled after Agents from the VALORANT Protocol. Each Agent has a different position along with 4 skills. Agents can be acquired through their contracts.
